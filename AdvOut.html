Advocacy and Outreach Committee

Mission

We work to educate the general public on the unparalleled scientific and cultural impact of Arecibo, as well as advocate for the future of Arecibo Observatory in order to fulfill its great promise to scientists worldwide, students and all levels, and the people of Puerto Rico.
